Vijender Singh meets Sachin Tendulkar for a unique reason

By akshay Bapat -
June 28, 2017
10
0
Share on Facebook
Tweet on Twitter

Indian professional boxer and Olympic bronze medalist Vijender Singh is set to lock horns with China’s undefeated southpaw Zulpikar Maimaitiali for a double title fight on 5th July in Mumbai.

Vijender, who is a WBO Asia Pacific Middleweight Champion, will battle Zulpikar, who is the WBO Oriental Super Middleweight Champion, at the NSCI Stadium in Worli, it was announced at a media conference, in the presence of the Indian boxing star.

Read: Vijender Singh wants to be patient before fighting for World Title

Vijender, the Beijing Olympics bronze medalist, has been training in Manchester, England, with his trainer Lee Beard for the big match, the first ticket of which was given to cricket icon Sachin Tendulkar at the latter’s home by Vijender himself. Vijender tweeted the photo of him presenting the ticket to the Master Blaster.

The very first ticket of my fight was presented to @sachin_rt . What a good start for us. Get ready for #BattleGroundAsia, everyone. pic.twitter.com/joYi2HGNME

— Vijender Singh (@boxervijender) June 27, 2017

In this fight, the two boxers will put their respective WBO titles on the line and whoever wins the bout will become a double champion, taking home the loser’s title with him.

Three other Indian boxers Akhil Kumar, Jitender Kumar and Neeraj Gayat will also be in action facing international competition, whose names are yet to be declared ,on the fight day and it will be 2006 Melbourne Commonwealth Games gold medalist Akhil’s debut bout as a professional pugilist.
